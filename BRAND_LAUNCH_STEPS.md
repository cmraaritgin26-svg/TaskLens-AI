# TaskLens AI Brand Launch Steps

This list is ordered by importance to the brand. Do one step at a time.

## 1. Lock The Brand Promise

Use this phrase as the center of the brand:

```text
Turn overwhelm into a clear next step.
```

Why it matters: people should understand TaskLens AI before they understand the features.

Link: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/13393723

## 2. Paste The Store Listing Copy

Use `STORE_LISTING_DRAFT.md` for the app name, short description, full description, screenshot captions, and reviewer instructions.

Where to do it: Google Play Console > your app > Store presence > Main store listing.

Link: https://play.google.com/console

## 3. Make The Screenshots Sell The Story

Create screenshots in this exact order:

1. Capture the overwhelm
2. Turn it into steps
3. Start with one
4. Track your wins
5. Keep the context

Why it matters: most people look at screenshots before reading the description.

Link: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9866151

## 4. Use The Current Icon And A Simple Feature Graphic

Keep the icon blue, calm, and recognizable. The feature graphic should show the app name and the core promise:

```text
TaskLens AI
Turn overwhelm into a clear next step.
```

Link: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9866151

## 5. Add Trust Signals

Use these links in the Play Console listing and policy sections:

- Privacy policy: `https://www.tasklensai.com/privacy-policy.html`
- Data deletion: `https://www.tasklensai.com/data-deletion.html`

Use this support email until a branded email exists:

```text
cmraaritgin26@gmail.com
```

Link: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/13628312

## 6. Keep The Website Simple

The repo already has a website at `website/index.html`. Keep it focused on:

- What the app does
- Screenshots or app preview
- Privacy policy
- Data deletion page
- Support email

When you are ready, publish it with GitHub Pages or another simple host.

GitHub Pages link: https://docs.github.com/en/pages/getting-started-with-github-pages/creating-a-github-pages-site

## 7. Avoid Risky Medical Claims

Use:

- focus-friendly
- scattered thoughts
- tasks that feel too big
- overwhelm
- practical next steps

Avoid:

- treats ADHD
- cures anxiety
- mental health treatment
- therapy language

Link: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9878810

## 8. Launch Internal Testing First

Upload the AAB to internal testing before production.

Use the current signed AAB:


Link: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/9845334

## 9. Ask For Early Honest Reviews

After the app is live, ask real users to leave honest reviews after they have used it.

Do not buy reviews or trade rewards for reviews.

Link: https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/138230
