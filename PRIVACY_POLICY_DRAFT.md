# Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 18, 2026

TaskLens AI is a personal task organization app. It helps users capture tasks,
organize them into Now, Next, and Later, create checklists, use focus timers,
and optionally generate AI-assisted task checklists from typed details or
photos.

## Information You Enter

The app may store information you choose to enter, including:

- Task names, notes, dates, deadlines, priority, size, and completion history
- AI checklist steps and checklist completion state
- Photos you choose to attach for AI checklist generation
- Settings, reminders, app lock preferences, and AI preferences
- Local backup/export data you create

## Local Storage

By default, app data is stored locally on your device. TaskLens AI does not
require an account to use the app. Guest mode is available.

## Optional App Lock

The app may offer optional local app lock features, including biometric/device
unlock or password protection. These features are used only to help protect
access to the app on your device.

## Optional Cloud AI

Cloud AI features are optional. They do not work unless you allow internet
sharing in setup or settings, enable AI checklist features, and configure an AI
backend URL.

If enabled, the app may send task titles, notes, dates, deadlines, priority,
size, typed details, checklist context, and attached photos over the internet
to the AI backend you configure. The backend uses this information to generate
task checklists and photo-based checklist suggestions.

AI checklists are suggestions and may be wrong. Review AI output before acting
on it. TaskLens AI is an organization and productivity tool.

## Backups and Exports

The app can export a backup file at your request. Exported backups may contain
task history, checklist data, settings, and other information you entered.
Store exported backups carefully.

Exported backups intentionally omit API keys, backend tokens, password hashes,
salts, and biometric credential IDs.

## Account and Data Deletion

You can delete local data using in-app delete controls, Settings > Master
reset, Android app storage clearing, or by uninstalling the app.

You can request deletion of any account-related or backend-accessible data at:

https://www.tasklensai.com/data-deletion.html

Most app data is stored locally on your device, so the developer cannot
remotely delete information that exists only on your phone.

## Security

The app supports guest mode, biometric/device locking, and password fallback
where biometric unlock is unavailable. Screenshots are allowed by the Android
app. No security measure is perfect, so you should protect your phone and avoid
sharing exported backups.
