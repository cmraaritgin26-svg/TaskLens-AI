Health & Task Tracker Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 8, 2026

Health & Task Tracker is a personal tracking app for tasks, nutrition, water, vitals, symptoms, mood, journal entries, reminders, dictation, and wellness trends.

Information You Enter

The app may store information you enter, including tasks and completion history, nutrition and water logs, weight, glucose, blood pressure, other vitals, symptoms, mood, notes, journal entries, reminders, app settings, app lock preferences, AI preferences, and dictation transcripts generated from speech.

Microphone Permission

The app requests microphone permission so you can use dictation. Dictation is used to turn speech into text and help fill in app fields. The app is not intended to save raw voice recordings. Dictation text and extracted information may be saved in the app if you confirm or use the dictated information.

Local Storage

By default, app data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for protecting your device, backups, and exported files.

Optional Cloud AI Features

Cloud AI features are optional. They do not work unless you allow internet sharing in setup or settings and configure the AI features. If enabled, the app may send health information, journal text, symptoms, mood, nutrition, vitals, tasks, notes, and dictation transcripts over the internet to the AI service you configure. The app uses this information for dictation extraction, trend summaries, and safety or wellness prompts.

Backups and Exports

The app can export a backup file at your request. Exported backups may contain health logs, journal entries, and other personal information, so store them carefully.

Security

The Android app blocks screenshots and recent-app previews while open. The app may support local password or biometric locking. No security measure is perfect, and the app is not a substitute for secure medical record systems.

Medical Disclaimer

The app is for personal tracking and wellness support. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease and does not replace professional medical advice. If you may hurt yourself or are in immediate danger, call or text emergency services where available.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13.
