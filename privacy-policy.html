Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 18, 2026

TaskLens AI is a focus-friendly photo checklist and task organization app. It helps users capture overwhelming tasks, organize them into Now, Next, and Later, create checklists, use focus timers, and optionally generate AI-assisted task checklists from typed details or photos.

Information You Enter

The app may store information you choose to enter, including:

  • Tasks and completion history
  • AI checklist steps and checklist completion state
  • Photos you choose to attach for AI checklist generation, plus optional after photos saved for progress comparison
  • Settings, reminders, app lock preferences, and AI preferences
  • Local backup/export data you create

Local Storage

By default, app data is stored locally on your device. TaskLens AI does not require an account to use the app. Guest mode is available.

Optional App Lock

The app may offer optional local app lock features, including biometric/device unlock or password protection. These features are used only to help protect access to the app on your device.

Optional Cloud AI

Cloud AI features are optional. They do not work unless you allow internet sharing in setup or settings and enable AI checklist features.

The app may use an included TensorFlow.js object detection model to scan selected photos on your device before the cloud AI request. This local scan helps identify common visible objects and does not upload data by itself.

If enabled, the app may send task titles, notes, dates, deadlines, priority, size, typed details, checklist context, selected before photos, and on-device TensorFlow labels over the internet to the TaskLens AI service. The service uses this information to generate task checklists and photo-based checklist suggestions.

After photos are saved locally with the task session for before/after comparison and are not needed for AI generation unless you choose to submit them in a later request.

AI checklists are suggestions and may be wrong. Review AI output before acting on it. TaskLens AI is an organization and productivity tool.

Backups and Exports

The app can export a backup file at your request. Exported backups may contain task history, checklist data, settings, and other information you entered. Store exported backups carefully.

Exported backups intentionally omit API keys, backend tokens, password hashes, salts, and biometric credential IDs.

The app can also export local AI feedback data at your request. This file may include task names, prompts, checklist text, edited checklist steps, completion state, and helpful/not helpful feedback. It does not include raw photo data.

Data Deletion

You can delete local data using in-app delete controls, Settings > Master reset, Android app storage clearing, or by uninstalling the app.

You can request deletion of any account-related or backend-accessible data at https://cmraaritgin26-svg.github.io/TaskLens-AI/data-deletion.html.

Most app data is stored locally on your device, so the developer cannot remotely delete information that exists only on your phone.

Security

The app supports guest mode, biometric/device locking, and password fallback where biometric unlock is unavailable. Screenshots are allowed by the Android app. No security method is perfect, so protect your phone and avoid sharing exported backups.
