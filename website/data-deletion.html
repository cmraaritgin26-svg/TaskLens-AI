Account and Data Deletion

Use this page to request deletion of any TaskLens AI account linkage and associated data that the developer can access.

Email contact@TaskLensAI.com with the subject TaskLens AI data deletion request.

Include the email address or Facebook account name you used with the app. Do not include sensitive details in the email.

What will be deleted

If any server-side account record, Facebook login link, support record, or AI/backend record is associated with your request, it will be deleted or anonymized where deletion is not technically possible.

Local data on your phone

Most TaskLens AI data is stored only on your device. The developer cannot remotely delete data that only exists on your phone. To remove local app data, use the in-app delete controls, Settings > Master reset, Android app storage clearing, or uninstall the app.

Timing

Deletion requests are reviewed and completed within 30 days when enough information is provided to identify the associated account or records.
